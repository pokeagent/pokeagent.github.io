Track 1: Competitive Battling

Pokémon battles represent one of the most complex zero-sum, partially observable games in existence, with an astronomical state space derived from over 1,000 Pokémon species, each with unique stats, abilities, typings, and items.

This track tests an agent's ability to operate under uncertainty, model opponents, and plan within time constraints, making it a prime benchmark for modern reinforcement learning and AI planning systems.

Core Challenges

  • Opponent modeling under partial observability
  • Stochastic decision-making with limited information
  • Strategic adaptation to novel teams and tactics
  • Strict per-turn action deadline (15 seconds)

Submission Guidelines

How to Submit for Track 1

Track 1 follows a two-phase evaluation process to ensure both robustness and competitiveness:

  • Phase 1: Open ladder using Pokémon Showdown’s matchmaking system to evaluate general strength
  • Phase 2: Bracket-style tournament between top teams for final placements

Your agent must interact with the Pokémon Showdown API and make decisions within a strict 15-second turn limit. Submission includes your agent's code, configuration, and logs.
